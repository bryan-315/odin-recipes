Old Fashioned

Description

This recipe shows how to make the Old Fashioned cocktail. There are many ways to craft this cocktail and this will simply be one of them.

The old fashioned is a bourbon-based cocktail that was created uhhh somewhere... im not sure where lol i think maybe New Orleans.

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • Angostura bitters
  • Bourbon of choice
  • Ice
  • 1 Orange
  • 1 bourbon cherry

Steps

  1. Add sugar cube to a mixing glass
  2. Add 4 dashes of Angostura bitters to the sugar cube
  3. Muddle the cube and bitters together to form a paste
  4. Add in 2 oz of Bourbon to the mixing glass
  5. Add ice and stir for 30 seconds
  6. Strain into an old fashioned or rocks glass with ice, preferrably only 1 large piece of ice
  7. Cut out an orange peel and squeeze out the zest onto the glass.
  8. Garnish with the orange peel and a bourbon cherry

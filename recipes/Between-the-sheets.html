Between the sheets

Description

This cocktail's origins are not set in stone but some say it is a derivative of the sidecar.

Ingredients

  • White rum or any light rum
  • Cognac
  • Triple sec or a different orange liqueur
  • Fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Chill a coupe, a Marie antoinette or a Nick & Nora glass
  2. Add 3/4 oz of lemon juice
  3. Add 1 oz of triple sec
  4. Add 1 oz of white rum
  5. Add 1 oz of cognac
  6. Fill the shaker with ice and shake for about 8-10 seconds
  7. Double strain the cocktail onto the chilled glass
